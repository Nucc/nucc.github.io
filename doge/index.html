Ball Dodge Game

Move your hands to control the colored balls and avoid the falling white balls!

Green ball = Right hand | Yellow ball = Left hand

Objective: Dodge white balls falling from the top

Lives: You start with 3 lives - lose one when any ball touches a white ball

Scoring: Earn 10 points for each white ball that passes safely

Difficulty: Game gets faster and spawns more balls as your score increases

Water ripples when you move and when balls collide!
