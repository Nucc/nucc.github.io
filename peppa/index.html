Peppa & George Adventure Game

Help Peppa and George collect dinosaurs while avoiding monsters!

🐷 Peppa (Pink) = Right hand | 🐷 George (Blue) = Left hand

Good Items: 🦕 Dinosaurs = +1 point each

Bad Items: 👹 Monsters = -1 life each

Lives: Start with 3 lives, lose when all lives are gone

Controls: Move both hands to control both characters

Difficulty: Items fall faster and spawn more frequently as you progress

Watch the water ripple as they move around!
